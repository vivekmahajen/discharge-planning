📋 Methodology & Data Sources

LOS Baseline

CMS FY 2026 IPPS Final Rule Table 5 — DRG geometric mean length of stay. Excess days = Actual LOS − DRG geometric mean LOS. Episodes without a DRG code cannot be included in savings calculations; cost impact shows $0 for those records.

Cost Per Day

Default $4,100/day (AHA 2024 data, California non-profit hospital average). Configurable per organization via ⚙ Configure → Cost Per Day. This figure represents the fully-allocated hospital cost (not charge) per inpatient day.

HRRP Penalty Estimate

~$4,500 per episode flagged for applicable HRRP conditions (AMI, CHF, COPD, Pneumonia, Hip/Knee arthroplasty, CABG). This is a conservative CMS-median estimate. Actual penalty depends on hospital-specific excess readmission ratios and total Medicare base operating DRG payments.

TCM Revenue

CPT 99495 ($163 facility rate) and 99496 ($228 facility rate) — CMS 2025 MPFS national non-facility rates. Requires documented care coordination contact within 2 business days of discharge and face-to-face visit within 7 (99496) or 14 (99495) days.

Annualized Run Rate

Projected by dividing total measured value by days elapsed, then multiplying by 365. Requires 10+ measured episodes for projection to display. Marked as estimate — actual outcomes depend on volume, payer mix, and DRG completeness.

Data Sources
