Methodology & Assumptions

  • CMS 2026 Physician Fee Schedule: CPT 99495 = $220.00 national avg, CPT 99496 = $298.00 national avg (ThoroughCare, February 2025)
  • California GPCI multiplier: 1.18 — Traditional Medicare rates: 99495 = $259.60, 99496 = $351.64
  • Medicare Advantage plans: national average rate (no GPCI uplift)
  • CPT code mix assumption: 70% CPT 99495 moderate complexity, 30% CPT 99496 high complexity
  • TCM-eligible = discharged to community (not SNF, LTACH, or hospice): 65% of Medicare discharges
  • CalAIM Enhanced Care Management: $122 per member per month (DHCS FY2025), 6-month typical engagement, ~20% of Medi-Cal managed care patients meet ECM criteria
  • Medi-Cal share of CA hospital discharges: ~28% (CA average)
  • CalAIM ECM is a separate CMS/DHCS program from Medicare TCM — different eligibility, workflows, and billing
  • CA hospital benchmark data: OSHPD CA Health & Human Services Open Data, 2023
Disclaimer: This calculator is illustrative. Actual revenue depends on payer contracts, provider enrollment, documentation completion, and geographic GPCI. CalAIM ECM revenue requires enrollment with your Medi-Cal managed care organization and a qualified Community Supports partner. Consult your billing team and compliance officer before projecting revenue changes. All numbers shown are estimates, not guarantees.
